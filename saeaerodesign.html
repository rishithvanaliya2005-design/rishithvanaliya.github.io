SAE Aero Design West 2025 — Team Arrow

Team Captain for SAE Aero Design West 2025 (Micro Class). Led design, fabrication, integration and testing of an autonomous competition UAV focusing on payload fraction, rapid assembly, and reliable flight performance.

Project Summary

SAE Aero Design West is an international competition testing airframe design, payload handling and mission capability. Our Micro Class entry prioritised a high payload fraction and quick assembly under strict power/weight limits.

  • Role: Team Captain — project management, design reviews, flight test sign-off.
  • Team: 30+ members across structures, avionics, propulsion and flight-test.
  • Key metrics: empty weight ≈ 1.6 kg, 2 L liquid payload, flight endurance > 5 min, takeoff < 10 ft, power limit 450 W.

Design & Engineering Notes

  • Airframe: lightweight composite/wood hybrid wings with optimized taper for efficient lift distribution.
  • Structure: quick-assembly spars and alignment features for reliable pre-flight checks.
  • Propulsion: powertrain tuned to stay within a 450 W limit while providing necessary climb and cruise margins.
  • Autonomy: waypoint-based missions with redundant sensors and pre-flight validation routines.
  • Testing: iterative flight tests + instrumented logging for control tuning and mission reliability.
