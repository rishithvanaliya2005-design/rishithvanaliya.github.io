CFD Lab · IIT Bombay

This work was carried out at the Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD) Lab, IIT Bombay under the guidance of Prof. Atul Sharma.

The lab works on a broad range of problems in multiphase flow physics, interface-capturing methods, heat transfer, and advanced computational fluid dynamics. The internship combined rigorous physical modeling with research-oriented scientific computing workflows.

Under Prof. Sharma’s guidance, I worked on level-set PINN formulations for passive bubble transport, static bubble dynamics, and bubble rise simulations, while comparing AD, ND, and CAN differentiation strategies for interface evolution problems.
