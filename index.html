About

Engineering at the intersection of fluid dynamics and machine learning

I’m a Mechanical Engineering graduate from Nirma University, graduating as department rank 1 with a 9.22 CGPA. My work focuses on computational fluid dynamics and Physics-Informed Neural Networks for fluid and thermal systems, with particular interest in multiphase flows, interface evolution, and simulation-driven modeling.

Alongside research, I spent three years building and leading UAV competition teams — progressing from design lead to team captain — developing aircraft that competed internationally at SAE Aero Design West in California.
