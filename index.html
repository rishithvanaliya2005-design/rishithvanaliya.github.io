About

Projects

Physics-Informed Neural Network Simulation of Breast Cancer Treatment via RFA

Integrating PINNs with OpenFOAM to simulate tumor response under Radiofrequency Ablation (RFA), optimizing thermal parameters for effective tissue destruction and predictive modeling.
