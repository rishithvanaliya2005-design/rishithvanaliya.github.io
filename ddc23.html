DDC Micro - 2023

Objective

  • Design a lightweight, belly‑landing fixed‑wing UAV for DDC Micro Class.
  • Maximise payload fraction while ensuring structural robustness and stable flight.
  • Meet DDC rules for hand‑launch, compact packaging, and short‑duration mission profile.

My Role - Design Engineer

  • Airframe layout, wing geometry selection, and structural sizing.
  • Material selection and weight‑build‑up optimisation.
  • Topology‑optimised wing structure and fuselage trade‑off study.
  • Contributed to aerodynamic decisions and stability validation using XFLR5.

Outcome

  • 2nd Place Overall - DDC Micro 2023
  • Best Design Report Award
  • Validated stable flight, high lift‑to‑drag performance, and robust belly‑landing behaviour.

Design Highlights

  • Cranked dihedral wing → passive roll stability with lower drag than conventional dihedral.
  • Tapered planform → reduced tip stall tendency.
  • Semi‑monocoque fuselage → strong, lightweight, easy to manufacture; foam belly for impact protection.
  • Structural optimisation → 40% weight reduction in wing ribs via topology optimisation.

Optimisation & Flight Performance

  • CFD‑based airfoil comparison → CH10 (root) + S1223 (mid) + AH79 (tip).
  • CFD results: Lift ≈ 31 N, Drag ≈ 1.88 N → L/D ≈ 16.4.
  • Wind‑tunnel CP measurement matched XFLR5 predictions. (See full report)
  • Wing‑loading test: 1.9 kg load caused only ~4.5 mm deflection.

Key Points & Lessons Learned

  • Cranked dihedral proved ideal for stability with minimal aerodynamic penalty.
  • Semi‑monocoque fuselage with XPS belly increased impact resilience.
  • Rigid wing‑mount design improved assembly speed and repeatability in field tests.
  • Topology optimisation offered major weight savings without compromising FOS.

Quick Specifications

  • Wing area: 0.296 m²
  • Aspect ratio: 6.4
  • Take‑off weight: 2.74 kg
  • Payload: 1.7 kg
  • Wing configuration: Cranked dihedral, tapered
  • Primary materials: Aeroply, balsa, XPS foam

Design Report - Condensed Summary

Aerodynamics

  • Airfoil combination chosen through CFD: CH10 (root), S1223 (mid), AH79 (tip).
  • Shoulder-mounted wing for better stability and protection during belly landings.
  • CFD validated pressure distribution and favorable Cp profile (see figures above).

Structures

  • Wing: balsa + aeroply ribs, dual shear webs; shear web placement tuned for load paths.
  • Topology optimisation reduced mass by ~104 g while preserving strength.

Fuselage

  • Square semi-monocoque selected for its ease of packaging and belly-landing resilience.
  • CFD and explicit-dynamic FEA informed shape and reinforcement placement.

Stability

  • Static margin ≈ 13.9% (within recommended 5-20%).
  • Longitudinal and most lateral modes stable; spiral mode slightly unstable but acceptable per report.

Testing & Validation

  • Wind-tunnel and tuft tests confirmed CFD predictions and stall characteristics.
  • Thrust bench verified propulsion sizing and current draw.

Propulsion

  • Motor: T-Motor AS2317 1400KV; Propeller: 10.25 x 6 inch; Battery: 3S 1500 mAh Li-Po.
  • Performance validated with bench tests and dynamic thrust tables in the report.
