Drone Design Challenge (DDC)

The SAE Drone Development Challenge (DDC) is a national-level UAV competition that challenges students to design, build, and demonstrate highly efficient unmanned aerial vehicles under strict dimensional and weight constraints.

The Micro Class focuses on maximizing payload fraction through lightweight structures, aerodynamic efficiency, and compact configurations. It encourages innovation in wing design, propulsion sizing, structural optimization, avionics integration, and mission reliability. DDC provides a hands-on platform for applying fluid dynamics, stability and control, structural mechanics, and systems engineering principles to build functional UAVs validated through real competition missions.
