Physics-Informed Neural Networks (PINNs)

Project summary: Implemented Fourier-based PINNs using PhysicsNeMo for 2D incompressible Navier–Stokes benchmark problems. Work focused on mesh-free learning, data-efficient supervision from CFD/experimental snapshots, and extracting wake frequency metrics.

Details

  • Problems solved: lid-driven cavity, cylinder wake (von Kármán shedding)
  • Data sources: Fluent & OpenFOAM snapshots (time windows selected post-stabilization)
  • Outputs: u/v/p fields, wake length, vortex shedding frequency
