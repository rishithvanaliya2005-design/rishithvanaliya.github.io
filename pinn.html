Physics-Informed Neural Networks (PINNs)

Project summary: Implemented Fourier-based PINNs using PhysicsNeMo for 2D incompressible Navier–Stokes benchmark problems and medical bioheat problems.

Research list

  • PINN — Navier–Stokes benchmark implementations
  • PINN — RF Ablation (Breast Cancer)

Details

  • Problems solved: lid-driven cavity, cylinder wake (von Kármán shedding), bioheat from RF ablation (Pennes equation).
  • Data sources: Fluent & OpenFOAM snapshots, FEM bioheat synthetic data.
  • Outputs: u/v/p or temperature fields, wake length, lesion prediction, and inferred tissue properties.
